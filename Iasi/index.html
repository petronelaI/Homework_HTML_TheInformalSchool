Iasi is the second city of Romania after Bucharest, in terms of population (371,889 residents as of 2018) and it is an important city in terms of culture, history and academic life. It is the second largest university center in Romania.

Iasi is a green city, loved by residents and visitors. Here you will find the most beautiful and impressive building in Romania, The Palace of Culture, the oldest and most beautiful university in Romania, Alexandru Ioan Cuza University, the most beautiful and oldest botanical garden in Romania, Botanical Garden "Anastasie Fătu", with the largest park roses, then the oldest and most beautiful national theater in Romania, The Vasile Alecsandri Theater, and the most beautiful mall in Romania, The Palas Mall, one of the best examples of the fact that the modern and the classic can stay together without disturbing the eyes of the passers-by. Built near the Palace of Culture The Palas Urban Assembly is included in the top of the most modern projects in Romania.
